## Scheduling

Consortium meetings should be held at least once a month, with the exact date decided by the Co-Presidents and Secretary as needed each month in order to accommodate the schedules of attendees. Upon scheduling consortium, the Secretary should send out a meeting reminder to all ASEC members and consortium attendees at least one (1) week before the scheduled meeting time.

## Guidelines

Consortium meetings are intended as a space for clubs to collaborate and share information, in order to promote cooperation and mutual support among the participating school clubs, address any issues or concerns, and avoid scheduling conflicts between club events. The Co-Presidents and Secretary should ensure that the meetings are held in a safe and inclusive environment for all attendees, and that all attendees feel comfortable sharing their thoughts and opinions.

## Attendance & Venue

Consortium meeting attendees include all ASEC members, the teacher supervisor of ASEC, as well as at least one (1) delegate from each school club (all clubs must send a delegate). The consortium should be held in a large room capable of fitting all attendees, with a projector, microphone, appropriate seating and optional podium.
