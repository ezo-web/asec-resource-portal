## Scheduling

At the beginning of each term, the Co-Presidents should decide what day of the week that internal meetings should be held on, in order to avoid scheduling conflicts with other activities for the ASEC members. 

**Note:** Internal ASEC meetings should run weekly. The Co-Presidents may change the day of the week that internal meetings are held on after the term starts in order to accommodate any changes, but they should not change the frequency of the meetings.

## Guidelines

Internal ASEC meetings are intended to be a space for the ASEC members to discuss any issues, events, policies and concerns, and to provide support to each other on their council-work. Internal meetings may also be used to provide updates on ASEC-invloved matters and review requests from the consortium.
These meetings are not intended to be held in a public forum, and are not to have spectators present (IE. no non-ASEC members should be present at internal meetings). The Co-Presidents and Secretary should ensure that the meetings are held in a safe and inclusive environment for all ASEC members, and that all members feel comfortable sharing their thoughts and opinions. Meeting reminders should be sent out via email to all ASEC members at least 24 hours before the scheduled meeting time, and the meeting agenda should be distributed at the start of the meeting.

The Secretary should log the meeting via the <a className="!text-foreground" href="https://forms.gle/kuA6xoctHrJJd4pJ6" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">ASEC Internal Meeting Logger Google Form</a>.

## Attendance

The only people who may be present during an internal ASEC meeting are the Co-Presidents, the Secretary, Grade Representatives, Communications Lead, Student Wellbeing Lead, and any official advisors. The teacher supervisor of ASEC may also attend if they with to do so. No quorum is required for an internal meeting to take place, however a meeting may be disbanded by the member(s) in charge of the meeting if they feel that there are not enough members present to have a productive meeting. If a member is unable to attend an internal meeting, they should notify the Co-Presidents or Secretary as soon as possible, and provide any relevant updates or information that they would like to be discussed during the meeting.
