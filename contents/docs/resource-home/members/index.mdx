Members are split into 3 catagories in order of seniority: Senior Leadership, Associate (Assoc.) Leadership, and Official Advisors.

## Senior Leadership

The highest ranked managing members of ASEC (based on administrative responsibilities and seniority). These members are responsible for tasks including high level decision making, overseeing the general operations of ASEC, providing support to the rest of the council, and/or representing a constituency.

## Official Advisors

The Official Advisors are a non-managing category of members within ASEC. They are responsible for providing support and advice to their respective council members, but don't make administrative decisions. They may perform certain tasks as delegated by their respective council members and approved by a Co-President.
