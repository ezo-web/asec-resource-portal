## Document Security Levels
| Security, High to Low | Level | Watermark Label | Description |
|-|-------|----|-------------|
| 1st | INT2 | INTERNAL COPY, DO NOT DISTRIBUTE, RETURN TO SECRETARY | Intended for meeting documents that are to be returned to the secretary at the end of the meeting |
| 2nd | INT1 | INTERNAL COPY, DO NOT DISTRIBUTE | Intended for general internal documents that shouldn't be distributed to people outside of ASEC |
| 3rd | PUB | PUBLIC COPY, DO NOT DISTRIBUTE | Intended for documents that are meant to be given to external parties and not distributed |
| 4th | REL | RELEASE COPY | Intended for publicly accessable documents which may be distributed freely outside of ASEC |

## Document Types
Most documents handled by ASEC fall into one of the categories in the table below. Most documents will be authored and distributed by the Secretary as the handler of ASEC's information services, though exceptions will be listed.

| Document | Security Level | Author | Distributor |
|-|-|-|-|
| Meeting Agenda | INT2 | Secretary/Co-Presidents | Secretary |
| Meeting Summary | INT1 | Secretary | Secretary |
| Meeting Notes | INT1 | Secretary | Secretary |
| Transparency Report | REL | Vice President | Co-Presidents |
| Monthly Audit | PUB | Secretary | Vice President |
| Consortium Presentation | INT1 | Secretary/Co-Presidents | Co-Presidents |
| Internal Policy | INT1 | Grade Representatives | Vice President |
| Handbook | INT1 | Secretary | Secretary |
